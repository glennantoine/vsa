Our Story

Aviation CTO was founded by a team of experienced aviation technology executives who recognized the need for specialized technology leadership in the aerospace and aviation industry. We saw that many aviation organizations couldn't afford a full-time CTO but desperately needed the specialized expertise.

What began as a consulting service for a handful of aviation clients has grown into a comprehensive technology leadership solution trusted by businesses across the aerospace sector. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality technology leadership that drives aviation business growth and success.

Today, we continue to expand our team of aviation technology experts and our service offerings, always with a focus on delivering exceptional value and helping our aviation clients navigate the complex intersection of aerospace and technology with confidence.

Our Mission & Values

At Aviation CTO, we are committed to helping aviation businesses leverage technology as a strategic asset. Our mission is to provide executive-level aviation technology leadership that aligns with aerospace business objectives and drives sustainable growth in this specialized industry.
